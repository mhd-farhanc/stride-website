STRIDE

A Step Tracker, Done Right

I'm proud to release Stride, a minimalist step tracker I built to practice my Flutter skills. My goal was a beautiful, private, and efficient app.

The step count resets every day, so you always start fresh and focused on today's movement.

🔒 Private by design. All of your health data stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded, shared, or tracked.

Built With Precision

Framework Flutter Cross-platform UI toolkit that powers Stride's smooth, native-feeling interface on every device.
Database Hive A lightweight on-device storage engine that keeps your step history fully local and offline.
Charts fl_chart Renders Stride's clean weekly and monthly activity graphs with zero lag.
Hardware Pedometer Reads live step data straight from your phone's built-in motion sensors.
Permissions permission_handler Gracefully requests only the activity access Stride needs, and nothing more.
